New Office in Arusha, Tanzania

New Office in Arusha, Tanzania

End World Hunger 2045 is delighted to announce the new partnership in Tanzania. The new office will be located in Arusha, Tanzania and will be operating with help of Pastor Credo. Our organization together with Pastor Credo will be supporting a school of 108 children. Pastor Credo is dedicated to helping the children of all walk of life and is commited even more with our help to make a beautiful place where children can learn and receive missing love from parents. Thank you Pastor Credo, for your dedication from all of us at End World Hunger 2045.
