WE ARE  END WORLD HUNGER 2045

End World Hunger 2045 is a non-profit foundation dedicated to helping provide those in need with life’s basic necessities such as food, clothing, and access to employment.

We are End World Hunger 2045, a different kind of non-profit organization. Our focus is on people we need to help. The latest statistics show there are over 820 million people are still hungry today despite all the wealth accumulated on Earth. Our view is not to donate more money, but to ensure what is donated is used and accounted for properly in order to create more employment opportunities in places like Africa, Eastern Europe, Asia and South America.

Our plan is to work with accountable and like minded organizations, people, and governments to create sustainability in places where we go.
